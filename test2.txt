Ebyo byona by'okora, oine omuanzi wawe, omugireyo okuhika ebyokurya, si kuwe, kw'Omukama, kw'Omukama. Baibuli neegamba ti, "Ebyo byona by'okora, bibikore n'omutima gwawe gwona, nk'eby'okora kuri Omukama." Naawe, omukazi, otera omubiri gwawe, tekikukwatako naawe, nka bakazi b'Omukama, ntaata, kino si kiwekyo kyange eri Eminem oba omuntu omulala, naawe omuanzi wange, kino kiwekyo kyange n'Omukama. Omukama wange, n'Omukama. Baibuli neegamba ti, "Ebyo byona eby'okukora, ebikomekere mu mutima, nk'eby'okukorera Omukama, si ku bubonero, oruga, orwa mugaso gw'okubeera omukulu, orwokuta, ky'obuta, oribeera omukulu." Oyo ow'okukorera mu butuufu ni Kristo Omukama.
